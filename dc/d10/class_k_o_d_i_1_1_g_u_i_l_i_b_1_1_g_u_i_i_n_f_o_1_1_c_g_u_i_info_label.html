Parse a provided label string into the list of info portions that may compose the label. Info portions mean the portions of complex infolabels along with their composition operations (and, or, etc) or skin variables. The label is also rewritten to resolve localized strings and other dynamic expressions.

Parameters
label[in, out] the string label in question (e.g. "$LOCALIZE[xxx]")
infoPortion[in, out] the list of info portions that may have been parsed from the string
contextthe context where the info expressions need to be evaluated (currently window ids)
